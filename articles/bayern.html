FC Bayern München

“Mia san mia” – The relentless pursuit of excellence

Born from the heart of Munich

Founded in 1900 by eleven young men led by Franz John, FC Bayern München grew from a local sports club into Germany’s greatest football dynasty. Its red colours and the Allianz Arena’s glow represent pride, precision, and the unyielding Bavarian spirit.

The dominance era

With icons like Beckenbauer, Müller, Lahm, and Neuer, Bayern defined European excellence. From the 1970s European Cups to the 2020 Treble, the club became synonymous with efficiency, leadership, and consistency.

Cultural identity

“Mia san mia” — we are who we are — is more than a motto. It’s a philosophy blending humility with ambition – a mentality that fuels Bayern’s dominance on and off the pitch.

Legacy

With an unmatched record in Germany and a global fanbase, Bayern continues to set standards in management, development, and sports culture. The club represents not just victory – but values.
